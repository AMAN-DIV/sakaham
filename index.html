समदृष्टि, क्षमाता विकास एवं अनुसंधान मंडल (सक्षम)

Dedicate to the service of visual impaired

सस्नेह नमस्कार, दिव्यांगता के क्षेत्र में सेवा और अनुसन्धान का संकल्प लेकर सक्षम याने समदृष्टि क्षमता विकास एवम् अनुसन्धान मंडल की स्थापना 20 जून 2008 को नागपूर में की गई।   सक्षम एक राष्ट्रीय संगठन है, जो दिव्यांगों को सर्वांगीण विकास एवं व्यावहारिक, सामाजिक, आर्थिक, राजनैतिक, सांस्कृतिक एवं आध्यात्मिक इत्यादी जीवन के विविध क्षेत्र में समरसता का अनुभव कराने हेतु कटीबद्ध है। साथ ही वह स्वावलम्बन, आत्म सम्मान तथा गरिमा के साथ जीवनयापन कर सके एवं राष्ट्र के पुनर्निर्माण मे अपनी सक्रिय भूमिका का निर्वहन कर सके, ऐसे वातावरण के निर्माण के लिए सक्षम प्रतिबद्ध है।   सभी दिव्यांगो तक अपनी पहुँच बनाने के लिए सक्षम अपने स्वरूप तथा कार्य-क्षेत्र को व्यापक विस्तार देने का प्रयास कर रहा है। राष्ट्रीय स्तर, प्रांत स्तर, जिल्हा स्तर पर अधिवेशन, संगोष्ठियां तथा कार्यशाला अदिका आयोजन करना साथ ही कला, क्रीडा, युवा, महिला, रोजगार, अॅडोकसी इन आयामों के माध्यम से निरंतर गतिशील है।

सक्षम

Samadrishti, Kshamata Vikas Evam Anusandhan Mandal (SAKSHAM) is a charitable national organization registered at Nagpur under registration number MAH/654/2008(N). The organization has its head office in Nagpur, Maharashtra. SAKSHAM was established with an aim to bring all the persons with various disabilities in the main stream of our nation. SAKSHAM believes that the disabled people are not burden on the society but assets of the nation. Though presently dedicated to the service of visually impaired, SAKSHAM has its commitment towards all the persons with various disabilities and also to environment, health and other social activities to strengthen the society.   • Visually Challenge

    A visual and hearing challenge means difficulty in seeing and hearing. These challenges can involve issues with clarity, depth perception, recognizing sounds, or understanding speech. Often, assistive tools or special techniques are needed to help manage these challenges effectively.

    Braille Displays and Note-Takers Assistive Listening Devices

  • Orthopaedic & Challenged

    Orthopedic challenges refer to physical limitations related to the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. These challenges can affect movement, strength, flexibility, and coordination, making daily activities difficult. People with orthopedic challenges often use assistive devices to improve mobility and independence. Here are some commonly used aids:

    Wheelchairs and Mobility Scooters Walkers, Crutches, and Canes Orthotic Devices Lift Chairs and Stairlifts

  • Blood Related Disabilities

    Blood-related disabilities are conditions that stem from abnormalities or disorders in the blood, affecting a person’s overall health, stamina, and ability to perform daily activities. Here are a few key examples

    Hemophilia Sickle Cell Anemia Thalassemia Anemia

  • Disability due to mental illness refers to impairments in a person’s ability to perform daily activities or maintain social relationships due to mental health conditions. These disabilities can affect cognition, mood, behavior, and perception, impacting one’s overall functioning. Here are some common examples

    Major Depressive Disorder Bipolar Disorder
